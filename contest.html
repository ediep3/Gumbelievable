Quarterly Bubble Gum Blowing Contest

Gumbelievable is proud to announce the first phase of the bubble gum blowing contest of 2022! The quarterly bubble gum blowing contest is an event that runs every three months, four times a year, referred to as a phase. Contestants will compete for rewards based on their performance at the end of each phase to score prizes and the chance to qualify for our largest competition, “The Big Bubble.”

Phases

Every year there will be four phases that run for approximately three months. Phase 1-3 is open to everyone, while Phase 4 will be a closed qualifier based on previous performance in the three phases.

Each phase, excluding the fourth, is independent of each other, meaning results from previous phases will not carry over and is not relevant to others in the future. However, your performance/scoring will determine if you have accumulated enough points to qualify for the fourth phase.

Phase 1: January 1st – March 23rd
Phase 2: April 1st – June 23rd
Phase 3: July 1st – September 23rd
Phase 4: October 8th – December 31st (The Big Bubble)

Structure

Contestants will compete against each other during each phase and score based on how big their bubble is blown. Measurement will be taken in inches using your tool of choice. The contestant must provide clear evidence for an entry to be valid.

Along with physical prizes, the top 25% of contestants in each phase will earn 20 points total, while the top 50% will earn 10 points instead.

To qualify for the fourth phase, contestants must have a minimum of 30 points. Your points will be added up and collected by us at Gumbelievable. We will release a list through Facebook and Instagram of contestants that qualify for the fourth phase.

Prizes

Winners will be announced 3-5 days after the phase has ended. The results will be posted on our Facebook and Instagram. We will reach out to the winners through email to collect their shipping information. There will be no charge to the winners as we cover all costs.

Please allow up to 1-2 weeks before receiving a tracking number after sending your shipping information to us. All orders will be shipped from Canada. Worldwide shipping is provided for this contest.

Phase 1-3 Prize Distribution:

  • 1st place: 5x Tub of Dubble Bubble Gum (1.44 KG each) + 5x Dubble Bubble Gumball Refill Carton (12 oz each)
  • 2nd place: 2x Tub of Dubble Bubble Gum (1.44 KG each) + 3x Dubble Bubble Gumball Refill Carton (12 oz each)
  • 3rd place: 1x Tub of Dubble Bubble Gum (1.44 KG each) + 2x Dubble Bubble Gumball Refill Carton (12 oz each)
  • Top 25%: 1x Dubble Bubble Gumball Refill Carton (12 oz each)

Phase 4 Prize Distribution:

  • 1st place: Personal Gum Ball Machine + 20x Dubble Bubble Gumball Refill Carton (12 oz each) + 10x Tub of Dubble Bubble Gum (1.44 KG each)

  • All Contestants: 1x Tub of Dubble Bubble Gum (1.44 KG each) + 2x Dubble Bubble Gumball Refill Carton (12 oz each)

Entry

You may submit an entry at any time during a phase. There is no limit to how many times you can enter as you might keep breaking your records.

To submit an entry, you must follow these requirements:

  • Follow us on either Facebook or Instagram

  • Submit your entry via Facebook or Instagram with the hashtags “#Gumbelievable” and “#TheBigBubble” along with the height and diameter of your bubble measured in inches with a ruler.

  • Include physical writing or a display of your name, a mention of Gumbelievable, and the date written somewhere in your picture or video entry. (Ex. On a piece of paper, drawing board, phone etc.).

  • Send the link of your post to our email (gumbelievablealberta@gmail.com) with the subject “Your Name – Phase 1 Contest Entry”

We wish you the best of luck during the contest. If you have any questions feel free to contact us on our social media networks or email. Happy blowing bubbles!

Whether you’re a candy veteran, we hope you learn and discover something new at Gumbelievable.
